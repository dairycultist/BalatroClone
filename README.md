roguelike deckbuilder (since I like those). you're a fat lady commander who's leading
their army into a quarantined zone, skirmishing with alien creatures, and attempting
to reach the center, the 'abyssal zone' (aka endgame). between skirmishes you visit
the armoury to buy stuff (has shopkeeper with snippy dialogue)

5x5 grid, on which you and your opponent have **pieces** that have an HP and an ATK value

on your turn you can move a piece, if you try to move a piece into an enemy piece your piece will attack theirs

at the armoury, you can buy/sell pieces and **equipment**. equipment are cards that you can assign to
pieces that modify the piece it is assigned to (any given equipment card can only be assigned to one
piece at a time).
- Sawed-off Shotgun "when attacked, +1 ATK"
- Illicit Substance "at start of round, +1 ATK and -1 HP"
- Scoped Rifle "Can attack, but not move to, diagonally adjacent tiles"
- Dead Man's Switch "If HP reaches 0, attacker gets -3 HP"
