5x5 grid, on which you and your opponent have **pieces** that have an HP and an ATK value

on your turn you can move a piece, if you try to move a piece into an enemy piece your piece will attack theirs

you can hold up to 5 cards; cards can be either **piece cards** (gives you a piece with a certain stat) or **ability cards** (balatro jokers, e.g. "when your piece is attacked, it gains +1 ATK"). you get a king piece for free without needing a card for it
